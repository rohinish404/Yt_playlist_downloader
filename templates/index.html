How to Download Playlist and Merge all the videos Step by Step

  1. Firstly, navigate to the YouTube website and find the playlist you want to download.
  2. Once you've found the playlist, copy its URL from the address bar at the top of your browser.
  3. Paste the playlist URL into the input field provided above besides the download button.
  4. Click the Download button to begin the process of merging and downloading all the videos in the playlist.
  5. Wait for the download to complete. Once finished, you'll have a single video file containing all the videos in the playlist, which will be saved to your system in the highest possible resolution.

How to Download Playlist videos separately into a zip file.

  1. Firstly, navigate to the YouTube website and find the playlist you want to download.
  2. Once you've found the playlist, copy its URL from the address bar at the top of your browser.
  3. Paste the playlist URL into the input field provided above, besides the download as zip button.
  4. Click the Download as Zip button to begin the process of downloading all the videos.
  5. Wait for the download to complete. Once finished, you'll have a zip file containing all the videos of the playlist that too in highest resolution possible.
